Welcome to DataDunks

As fans of Data and Basketball, we wanted to use data to predict the outcome of a basketball match in a simulated (what-if) scenario. To extend the meaningfulness of the project, we incorporated two modes (Player vs Player) and (Team vs Team), and gave users access to the data used within the project. Click the links below to get more information. Choose to either visit our GitHub Documentation page or to be redirected to the "About Us" page that houses our intentions and initial project proposal.
